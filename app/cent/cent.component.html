Cent Account

  • The main distinctive feature of cent (micro) accounts is that they use 100x denominated monetary units of the base currency (US Cent, EU Cent, etc.), while the trading conditions are equal to those for standard accounts.
    Just deposit 10 USD to your cent account to get 1,000 US Cents. As a result, this account types will be excellent not only for beginner traders, but for professionals as well, because it may help them to test new trading strategies or Expert Advisers.
